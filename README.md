## Day 1 — Sun, Jun 7 · Mula (Sultanahmet) · Arrival
- **13:55** Land IST.
- **13:55–15:15** Passport control + baggage (budget 60–90 min). Buy IstanbulKart.
- **15:15** → Mula Hotel: TPT car/Uber/taxi ~45–60 min, or M11 → Gayrettepe → M2 → Vezneciler + tram (~75–90 min).
- **16:30** Check in, freshen up.
- **17:30** Sultanahmet Square / Hippodrome, German Fountain, Blue Mosque exterior. (Sunset ~20:35.)
- **19:30** Rooftop dinner (Hagia Sophia view); Hafız Mustafa dessert.
- *Tickets: none. Change a little cash in the city, not the airport.*

## Day 2 — Mon, Jun 8 · Mula · Old City monuments (all walkable)
*Monday on purpose — Topkapı closes Tuesdays.*
- **08:30** Walk to Hagia Sophia (opens 09:00). *Foreigner upper gallery ~€25, book online.*
- **10:30** Topkapı Palace + Harem (2.5–3 hrs). *Palace + Harem add-on, muze.gov.tr.*
- **13:30** Lunch by Gülhane Park.
- **14:30** Basilica Cistern (45 min). *Timed ticket, book online.*
- **16:00** Blue Mosque interior (free; closed during 5 prayer windows; shoulders/knees covered, scarf for women).
- **17:00** Süleymaniye Mosque (free; best terrace view in the city).
- **19:30** *Optional:* Çemberlitaş Hamamı (1584) or local dinner.

## Day 3 — Tue, Jun 9 · Move to Hilton Maslak · Bazaars + Asian side
- **08:00** Breakfast; check out of Mula, leave luggage at reception.
- **09:00** Grand Bazaar (opens 08:30; haggle hard).
- **11:00** Spice Bazaar via Rüstem Pasha Mosque (free, stunning tiles). Buy Turkish delight (sampled, cut & vacuum-sealed, by weight).
- **12:30** Lunch at Eminönü.
- **13:30** Ferry Eminönü → Kadıköy (~20 min, IstanbulKart — scenic crossing).
- **14:00** Kadıköy market + Moda waterfront.
- **15:30** *Optional:* taxi to Grand Çamlıca Mosque (panoramic views) — skip to lighten the day.
- **17:30** Sunset ferry back Kadıköy → Eminönü.
- **18:30** Tram T1 → Sultanahmet; collect luggage from Mula.
- **19:30** Taxi/Uber → Hilton Istanbul Maslak (~40–50 min). Check in; dinner near hotel.

## Day 4 — Wed, Jun 10 · Hilton Maslak · WEDDING DAY 
- **Morning:** relax — breakfast, pool/spa, light lunch. (Long night ahead.)
- *Optional light outing (back by ~2:00):* coffee in Emirgan / Emirgan Park (~10 min away).
- **15:00** Start getting ready. **Formal — no white or green.**
- **16:15** Taxi/Uber to Sait Halim Paşa Yalısı, Yeniköy (~15–25 min; coastal Köybaşı Cd. can jam). **No need to leave at 3:30 — venue is near your hotel.**
- **17:00** Ceremony → cocktail 6 → reception 7 → dinner 8:30 → dancing 9:30. Taxi back to Maslak after.

## Day 5 — Thu, Jun 11 · Hilton Maslak · City sightseeing → Paiwazi boat
- **10:30** M2 from İTÜ–Ayazağa → Taksim → funicular to Kabataş, or taxi (~25–30 min) to Dolmabahçe.
- **11:00** Dolmabahçe Palace (open Thu; ~2 hrs). *Ticket onsite or muze.gov.tr.*
- **13:30** Lunch in Beşiktaş, then Ortaköy waterfront (mosque + famous kumpir/waffles).
- *Alt:* swap for Galata Tower + İstiklal (M2 to Şişhane/Taksim).

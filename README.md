# Istanbul Itinerary - June 7-12, 2026

**Trip:** Zainab & Aneal's wedding (Sait Halim Paşa Yalısı, Yeniköy)
**Hotels:** Mula Hotel, Sultanahmet (Jun 7-8) -> Hilton Istanbul Maslak (Jun 9-11)

## Flights (Air Transat)
- **Outbound TS 214:** Sat Jun 6, 9:00 PM YYZ (Terminal 3) -> **Sun Jun 7, 1:55 PM IST** (non-stop)
- **Inbound TS 215:** **Fri Jun 12, 3:45 PM IST** -> 7:45 PM YYZ (Terminal 3, non-stop)

## Wedding events
- **Jun 10 - Wedding** @ Sait Halim Paşa Yalısı, Yeniköy, Köybaşı Cd. No:83, 34464 Sarıyer
  - Ceremony **5:00 PM** -> Cocktail 6:00 PM -> Reception 7:00 PM -> Dinner 8:30 PM -> Dancing 9:30 PM
  - Dress: **Formal. NO white, NO green.** Use the couple's palette.
- **Jun 11 - Paiwazi / Kangan boat** @ "Kaptan Boran" (confirm dock)
  - Boarding ~5:30 PM, **departs 6:00 PM prompt, returns 11:00 PM**
  - Dress: **Semi-formal. NO red.** Bring a light jacket (chilly on the water).

**First thing on arrival:** buy an **IstanbulKart** at the airport (metro, tram, ferry, funicular; up to 5 taps/card).

## Day 1 - Sun, Jun 7 - Mula (Sultanahmet) - Arrival
- **1:55 PM** Land IST.
- **1:55-3:15 PM** Passport control + baggage (budget 60-90 min). Buy IstanbulKart.
- **3:15 PM** -> Mula Hotel: TPT car/Uber/taxi ~45-60 min, or M11 -> Gayrettepe -> M2 -> Vezneciler + tram (~75-90 min).
- **4:30 PM** Check in, freshen up.
- **5:30 PM** Sultanahmet Square / Hippodrome, German Fountain, Blue Mosque exterior. (Sunset ~8:35 PM.)
- **7:30 PM** Rooftop dinner (Hagia Sophia view); Hafız Mustafa dessert.
- *Tickets: none. Change a little cash in the city, not the airport.*

## Day 2 - Mon, Jun 8 - Mula - Old City monuments (all walkable)
*Monday on purpose - Topkapı closes Tuesdays.*
- **9:30 AM** Hagia Sophia (opens 9:00 AM). *Foreigner upper gallery ~€25, book online.*
- **11:00 AM** Topkapı Palace + Harem (2.5-3 hrs). *Palace + Harem add-on, muze.gov.tr.*
  - Walk ~5 min: [Hagia Sophia -> Topkapı Palace](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Hagia+Sophia,Istanbul&destination=Topkapi+Palace,Istanbul&travelmode=walking)
- **2:00 PM** Lunch by Gülhane Park.
  - Walk ~6 min: [Topkapı Palace -> Gülhane Park](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Topkapi+Palace,Istanbul&destination=Gulhane+Park,Istanbul&travelmode=walking)
- **3:00 PM** Basilica Cistern (45 min). *Timed ticket, book online.*
  - Walk ~7 min: [Gülhane Park -> Basilica Cistern](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Gulhane+Park,Istanbul&destination=Basilica+Cistern,Istanbul&travelmode=walking)
- **4:30 PM** Blue Mosque interior (free; closed during 5 prayer windows; shoulders/knees covered, scarf for women).
  - Walk ~5 min: [Basilica Cistern -> Blue Mosque](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Basilica+Cistern,Istanbul&destination=Blue+Mosque,Istanbul&travelmode=walking)
- **5:30 PM** Süleymaniye Mosque (free; best terrace view in the city).
  - Walk ~18 min: [Blue Mosque -> Süleymaniye Mosque](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Blue+Mosque,Istanbul&destination=Suleymaniye+Mosque,Istanbul&travelmode=walking)
- **7:30 PM** *Optional:* Çemberlitaş Hamamı (1584) or local dinner.
- **9:00 PM** Evening stroll through Sultanahmet Square to see Hagia Sophia and the Blue Mosque illuminated; finish with Turkish tea or apple tea at a nargile (hookah) lounge nearby.

## Day 3 - Tue, Jun 9 - Move to Hilton Maslak - Bazaars + Asian side
- **8:30 AM** Breakfast; check out of Mula, leave luggage at reception.
- **9:30 AM** Grand Bazaar (open; haggle hard).
  - Walk ~14 min: [Mula Hotel -> Grand Bazaar](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Mula+Hotel+Sultanahmet,Istanbul&destination=Grand+Bazaar,Istanbul&travelmode=walking)
- **11:30 AM** Spice Bazaar via Rüstem Pasha Mosque (free, stunning tiles). Buy Turkish delight (sampled, cut & vacuum-sealed, by weight).
  - Walk ~10 min: [Grand Bazaar -> Spice Bazaar](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Grand+Bazaar,Istanbul&destination=Spice+Bazaar,Istanbul&travelmode=walking)
- **1:00 PM** Lunch at Eminönü.
  - Walk ~3 min: [Spice Bazaar -> Eminönü ferry pier](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Spice+Bazaar,Istanbul&destination=Eminonu+Ferry+Terminal,Istanbul&travelmode=walking)
- **2:00 PM** Ferry Eminönü -> Kadıköy (~20 min, IstanbulKart - scenic crossing).
- **2:30 PM** Kadıköy market + Moda waterfront.
- **4:00 PM** *Optional:* taxi to Grand Çamlıca Mosque (panoramic views) - skip to lighten the day.
- **6:00 PM** Sunset ferry back Kadıköy -> Eminönü.
- **7:00 PM** Tram T1 -> Sultanahmet; collect luggage from Mula.
- **8:00 PM** Taxi/Uber -> Hilton Istanbul Maslak (~40-50 min). Check in; dinner near hotel.

## Day 4 - Wed, Jun 10 - Hilton Maslak - WEDDING DAY
- **9:30 AM** *Optional light outing:* coffee/stroll at Emirgan Park (~10 min away); back by ~12:00 PM. Otherwise relax - breakfast, pool/spa. (Long night ahead.)
- **3:00 PM** Start getting ready. **Formal - no white or green.**
- **4:15 PM** Taxi/Uber to Sait Halim Paşa Yalısı, Yeniköy (~15-25 min; coastal Köybaşı Cd. can jam). **No need to leave at 3:30 - venue is near your hotel.**
- **5:00 PM** Ceremony -> Cocktail 6:00 PM -> Reception 7:00 PM -> Dinner 8:30 PM -> Dancing 9:30 PM. Taxi back to Maslak after.

## Day 5 - Thu, Jun 11 - Hilton Maslak - City sightseeing -> Paiwazi boat
- **9:30 AM** M2 from İTÜ-Ayazağa -> Taksim -> funicular to Kabataş, or taxi (~25-30 min) to Dolmabahçe.
- **10:00 AM** Dolmabahçe Palace (open Thu; ~2 hrs). *Ticket onsite or millisaraylar.gov.tr.*
- **12:30 PM** Lunch in Beşiktaş.
  - Walk ~10 min: [Dolmabahçe Palace -> Beşiktaş](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Dolmabahce+Palace,Istanbul&destination=Besiktas,Istanbul&travelmode=walking)
- **1:30 PM** Ortaköy waterfront (mosque + famous kumpir/waffles).
  - Walk ~25 min along the water (or short taxi/bus): [Beşiktaş -> Ortaköy](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Besiktas,Istanbul&destination=Ortakoy,Istanbul&travelmode=walking)
- *Alt:* swap for Galata Tower + İstiklal (M2 to Şişhane/Taksim).
- **4:30 PM** Head to "Kaptan Boran" boarding point; be there 5:30 PM.
- **6:00 PM** Depart promptly - returns 11:00 PM. **Semi-formal, no red.** Light jacket. Taxi back to Maslak after.

## Day 6 - Fri, Jun 12 - Departure (TS 215, departs IST 3:45 PM)
- **8:00 AM** Final Turkish breakfast.
- **10:45 AM** Check out.
- **11:30 AM** Hilton Maslak -> IST: taxi ~35-45 min, or M2 (İTÜ-Ayazağa) -> Gayrettepe -> M11 to IST (~45-55 min).
- **12:45 PM** At airport (≈3 hrs before international flight).
- **3:45 PM** Depart -> arrive YYZ 7:45 PM (Terminal 3).
- *No morning sightseeing - Friday mosques open to tourists only after ~1:00 PM anyway.*

- Whirling Dervish (Sema) ceremony - unique evening show.

## Getting around cheat sheet
- **IstanbulKart** for metro/tram/ferry/funicular.
- **Uber or BiTaksi** for cabs (match plate; check fare lands in the quoted range).
- **Hilton Maslak <-> city:** M2 "İTÜ-Ayazağa" -> Taksim (İstiklal) / Şişhane (Galata) / Vezneciler (old city), ~30-40 min.
- **Airport (IST):** M2 -> Gayrettepe -> M11, ~45 min.
- **Cash:** exchange small amounts at street stalls in the city, never the airport.
- **Restaurants:** skip places without posted prices; "tasting" plates aren't free; cash = no service fee/tax.
